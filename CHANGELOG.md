## [0.1.0] - 2026-01-03
### Added
- Initial release of the FAST Method documentation.
- Core Philosophy section defining meetings as the "Last Resort" and establishing the four Immutable Tenets.
- "Why FAST?" section comparing the method to traditional meetings and alternative frameworks.
- The FAST Framework breakdown: Filter (The Who), Agenda (The What), Shorten (The When), Take Action (The Output).
- "Mechanics: The FAST Ticket" section detailing calendar invite requirements (Goal, Cost, Pre-read, Output).
- Integrity Check guidelines for meeting organizers.
- "Where is the Discussion?" section defining the separation of written discussion and synchronous conflict resolution.
- Implementation examples for Software and Hardware contexts.
- Common Questions section addressing exclusion, silent starts, and general discussion.
- Abbreviations section.
